Design and development of a dismantle-able semi rigid airship

Junior Research Fellow, Lighter-Than-Air Systems Lab, IIT Bombay, India
September 2014 – June 2015
Principle Investigator: Prof. Rajkumar Pant, IIT Bombay


  • Designed and built a prototype of remotely controlled semi-rigid airship with a dismantle-able frame to provide structural strength and ability to mount propulsion units on off-gondola locations
  • Publication: S. Loharkar, A. Kshirsagar, R.S. Pant, “Design and Fabrication of a portable semi-rigid airship”, Annual Technical Volume of Aerospace Engineering Division Board, Institution of Engineers (India), 2015-16 Link
