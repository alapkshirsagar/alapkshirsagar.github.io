Tracking and control of UR-5 manipulator using iGPS and ROS

Master's thesis at Institut für Getriebetechnik und Maschinendynamik (IGM), RWTH Aachen, Germany
October 2016 - Present
German Advisor: Univ.-Prof. Burkhard Corves, RWTH Aachen
Indian Advisor: Dr. Sourav Rakshit, IIT Madras

  • Part of 'Center of Advanced Robotics (COAR)' group at IGM
  • Designing an algorithm for tracking end effector of Universal Robot-5 (UR5) using indoor GPS system
  • Developing packages for control of UR5 end effector in Robot Operating System (ROS)
  • Project involves evaluation of tracking and control algorithms on ‘Gazebo’ model and actual UR5 robot
