Design of Fabric Cutting Machine for Mat-making Handlooms

Student Investigator, Rural Technology Advancement Group (RuTAG), IIT Bombay
January 2012- November 2013
Principle Investigator: Prof. Suhas Joshi, IIT Bombay


  • As a part of 3 member team: Designed, fabricated and tested various prototypes of human powered as well as electric fabric cutting machine to increase the productivity of mat-making handlooms operated by visually challenged people
  • Awarded Gandhian Young Technological Innovation Award by Society for Research and Initiatives for Sustainable Technologies and Institutions Details
